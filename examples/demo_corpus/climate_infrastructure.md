# Climate Infrastructure

Flood response teams combine rainfall forecasts, river gauge readings, threshold crossings, and
evacuation status to decide when a community warning should become an evacuation workflow.

A resilient microgrid pairs battery storage with solar generation and islanding controls so that
critical facilities can keep serving residents during a grid outage.

Heat-response planning links cooling centers, outreach teams, vulnerability maps, and weather
forecasts before a dangerous heat wave reaches high-risk residents.
